Nice to meet you. My name's Sam.

I'm a 19-year-old student entering my third year at Princeton University and pursuing a degree in economics and a minor in computer science.

This is what I can do.

I am professionally-proficient in java and C and am currently developing skills in HTML, CSS, and javascript. My previous work experience also includes internships in education-consulting and private equity analysis. My professional aspirations are to work as an entrepenuer and eventually work in private equity as an investor. My background on both the investment and entrepenuerial side will serve me well in this field. I am also fluent in Spanish.

Here's my story.

A Texan born and bred, I grew up in suburban Dallas with two older brothers and a younger sister and went to St. Mark's School of Texas for 12 years. Outside of class, I'm the president of the Princeton Club Basketball Team and participate in various community service projects including Habitat For Humanity. I am also a member of the Kappa Alpha Order and the University Cottage Club, and the former president of the Princeton Texans Club. I am an avid sports fan and supporter of all my hometown teams in Dallas, Texas as well as Arsenal Football Club.
